Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common queries about our digital marketing services.

1. What is Digital Marketing?
Digital marketing includes online strategies like SEO, PPC, SMM, content marketing & analytics.
2. What is SEO and why is it important?
SEO improves Google ranking, increases visibility, and brings organic customers without ads.
3. What is PPC advertising?
PPC means you pay only when someone clicks your ad — powerful for fast lead generation.
4. What is CRM and how does it help?
CRM helps track leads, manage customers, boost conversions & ensure no lead is missed.
5. Do I need both SEO and PPC?
Yes — SEO builds long-term trust; PPC brings quick results. Both combined = best results.
6. How long does it take to see results?
SEO: 3–6 months
Social Media: 1–2 months
PPC Ads: Immediate
7. Will you manage my social media accounts?
Yes — we handle content creation, posting, engagement & reporting.
8. Do you design and develop websites?
Yes — responsive, SEO-friendly & high-conversion websites for all industries.
9. How do you measure performance?
Via Analytics, CRM dashboards, Meta Insights & monthly reports.
10. Can you run ads for my business?
Yes — Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram & YouTube campaigns.
11. Do you offer customized packages?
Yes — tailored strategies based on goals, competition & budget.
12. What industries do you specialize in?
Healthcare, real estate, education, retail, personal brands & more.
13. Do I need a long-term contract?
No — flexible monthly & project-based plans available.
14. How do I get started?
Contact us via WhatsApp, call, or contact form.
