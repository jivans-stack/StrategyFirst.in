Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear and concise answers to the queries we receive most often about our digital marketing services.

1. What is Digital Marketing?
Digital marketing includes online strategies such as SEO, PPC advertising, social media marketing, content marketing, and performance analytics.
2. What is SEO and why is it important?
SEO improves your website’s visibility on Google, increases organic traffic, and helps attract customers without relying on paid ads.
3. What is PPC advertising?
PPC (Pay-Per-Click) is a paid advertising model where you are charged only when someone clicks your ad. It is ideal for generating fast leads and traffic.
4. What is a CRM and how does it help?
A CRM system helps track leads, manage customer interactions, streamline follow-ups, and increase conversion rates by ensuring no lead is missed.
5. Do I need both SEO and PPC?
Yes. SEO builds long-term authority, while PPC provides immediate results. Combining both delivers the strongest overall performance.
6. How long does it take to see results?
SEO: 3–6 months
Social Media Growth: 1–2 months
PPC Ads: Immediate
7. Will you manage my social media accounts?
Yes. We manage content creation, posting, engagement, audience growth, and monthly performance reporting.
8. Do you also design and develop websites?
Absolutely. We build responsive, SEO-friendly, and high-conversion websites tailored to your brand and industry.
9. How do you measure performance?
We track performance using Google Analytics, CRM dashboards, Meta Insights, and detailed monthly reports.
10. Can you run ads for my business?
Yes. We manage Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, and YouTube advertising campaigns.
11. Do you offer customized packages?
Yes. We create tailored marketing packages based on your goals, competition level, and budget.
12. Which industries do you specialize in?
We work with businesses in healthcare, real estate, education, retail, hospitality, personal brands, and many more sectors.
13. Do I need a long-term contract?
No. We offer flexible monthly plans and project-based options based on your requirements.
14. How do I get started?
Simply contact us via WhatsApp, phone call, or submit your details through the contact form.
